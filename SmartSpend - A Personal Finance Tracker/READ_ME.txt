SmartSpend is a simple command-line based expense tracker written in C.
It helps you keep track of your daily expenses by allowing you to add, view, and modify your spending records.

✨ Features
✅ Add an expense (with date, category, and amount)
📄 View all recorded expenses
🔍 Filter expenses by category
✏️ Modify an expense by category (update date, amount, and category)

All expense records are saved in a text file (expenses.txt) for simplicity.

🛠 How to Run
1. Run It directly in Vs-Code 
or
In CMD(Command Prompt)-
1. gcc expense_tracker.c -o tracker
2../tracker
